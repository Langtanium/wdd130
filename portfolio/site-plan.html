3d Animation

What I do

I create 3d models and rig them, and I have experience with creating shaders for the models. I also make renders and have experience with making photorealistic and stylized renders of the 3d models.

Phone image description

This is a 3d model of a fictional cellphone that makes an appearance in a game called Splatoon. This project helped me with getting experience on how to do hard surface modeling and making a photorealistic product.

Programs I use

I currently use Blender because of its easy access and high capability, but I won't mind having to use a different program such as Maya. I also use Blockbench to make cubic styled models, and I use Gimp and Krita for editing textures, and I have used Photoshop in the past.
